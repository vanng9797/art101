P5JS Project: K/DA Music Visualizer

Interface and Language Track

This is my final project for ART101 Creative Coding class, which I focus on making a visualizer for a music track of the virtual Kpop girl band called K/DA from the famous game League of Legend by Riot Games. I think that each instrumental track can have its own form of visual. For example, in my project, I used K/DA's song "The Baddest" and splitted the audio track into individuals, for example: drum, bass, synth, vocals, etc. Then, for each of them, I map their amplitude to the visuals, so that each scene has a unique representation for a specific instrumental layer. I also cut some short samples that are used in the track to make some add-on sound effects and to create animations for them. I hope users/ players can learn more about sound design and have fun playing around with the instrumental tracks.
